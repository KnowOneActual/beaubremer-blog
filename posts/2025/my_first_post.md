## Welcome! I'm new here (and I have no idea what I'm doing)

Well, I did it. I finally have a new online home. Welcome!

Right now, this space is a work in progress. It is a bit like me on a Monday morning: slightly confused, but full of
potential. I am working behind the scenes to untangle technical knots and learn how this works. (If you hear faint
screaming, it is just me trying to center an image.)

I won't accidentally delete the entire internet.

Please look around and ignore any digital potholes. Thank you for being here at the very start. Let's get this show on
the road!
